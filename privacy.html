EarningsHQ Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

EarningsHQ is an iOS app that helps Google AdSense publishers view their earnings data. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we access and how we handle it.

Data We Access

EarningsHQ requests read-only access to your Google AdSense account to display your earnings, payments, and performance metrics. We access this data solely to show it to you within the app.

Data We Store

EarningsHQ stores the following data locally on your device:

• Your Google authentication token (to keep you signed in)
• Your notification preferences and alert settings
• Your theme preferences

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit your earnings data, personal information, or Google account credentials to any server. We have no backend servers and no database. All data stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

EarningsHQ uses the following third-party services:

• Google Sign-In — for authentication with your Google account
• Google AdSense API — to retrieve your earnings data (read-only)
• Google AdMob — to display advertisements (free tier only)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Advertising

The free version of EarningsHQ displays interstitial ads via Google AdMob. EarningsHQ Pro subscribers do not see ads. Ad networks may collect device identifiers for ad personalization per their own policies.

Children's Privacy

EarningsHQ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at myearningshq@gmail.com.
